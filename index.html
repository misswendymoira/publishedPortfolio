About Me

Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Wendy who loved to paint.

(That's me!)

I was eight years old when I began painting murals for restaurants, offices, people's homes, and even public schools. My work can be found all over Northern California!

However, living in the San Francisco Bay Area was very expensive, so when I was fifteen, I also took on my first "regular" job. While I have continued to paint through the years, I have also garnered an impressive professional resume, ranging from kayaking instructor to manager in a corporate setting.

I have now begun a new chapter here in Portland, Oregon, where I can integrate my years of professional experience with my passion for design!

Project Examples

I have experience with all aspects of UX, from research and ideation to user testing and data synthesization. However, my backround has been centered around artistic pursuits, which have greatly influenced my design capabilities, leading to a newfound passion for UI.

































When working in team settings, I am often assigned supervision of high-fidelity prototypes, as I have knowledge regarding HTML, CSS, and Javascript, allowing me to ensure pixel-perfection which will inform how the development team translates prototypes into functional, live web pages.

As I continue to gain knowledge and experience,
I will share more projects and insights here!
